About Jack

The builder behind Built By Budge.

I’m Jack Budge, an engineer, designer and founder focused on building tools and experiences that actually help people: healthier bodies, clearer minds and more playful lives.

I design and code my own products end-to-end, from architecture and physics to typography and animations. Built By Budge is the banner for all of those experiments.

  • Mechanical engineering background with a bias toward shipping.
  • Building production-grade apps in Flutter and native iOS.
  • Releasing records and projects like GIRL and Experiment 42.
  • Based in New Zealand, building for a global audience.
