Health

Overview

One intelligent platform for your whole health.

NOVA is a holistic health operating system. It helps with running, gym, sleep, groceries, cooking, food analysis, cycling, yoga, bloodwork, journaling, debating and more. It uses AI to surface insights, routines and gentle nudges that fit your life. Instead of juggling disconnected apps, NOVA becomes the single place where your health story lives. NOVA does not track or sell your data, everything stays safely on your device.
