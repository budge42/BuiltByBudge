Health

Overview

One intelligent layer over your health – connecting data, habits and decisions in real time.

NOVA is a holistic health operating system. It tracks movement, running, training, sleep, food, mood and more – then uses AI to surface insights, routines and gentle nudges that actually fit your life. Instead of juggling a stack of disconnected apps, NOVA becomes the single place where your health story lives.
